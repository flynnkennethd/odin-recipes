Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A simple yet delicious grille cheese sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Your choice of cheese

Directions

  1. Heat a pan and melt a small amount of butter in it
  2. Place two pieces of bread face-down in the pan
  3. After they have been cooked, flip them
  4. Add cheese to each piece of bread
  5. Once the other side of the bread is cooked, put the two cheesy sides together and transfer to a plate
